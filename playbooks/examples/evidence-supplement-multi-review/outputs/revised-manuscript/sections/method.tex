\section{Method}
Our model uses a transformer encoder to fuse image tokens with spectral descriptors before classification.

\subsection{Ablation Setup}
We added an ablation summary showing that removing either the image branch or the spectral branch weakens performance, indicating that the gain comes from complementary cross-modal evidence rather than a single dominant stream.

\subsection{Training Setup}
We use a fixed train/validation/test split of 640/160/200 plots and repeat all stochastic experiments with seeds 11, 17, 23, 29, and 37.
