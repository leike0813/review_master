\begin{abstract}
Sequence transduction models are usually built from recurrent or convolutional components together with attention. We study a simpler architecture, the Transformer, that uses attention throughout the encoder and decoder. On WMT 2014 English-to-German and English-to-French translation, the model achieves stronger BLEU scores than competitive baselines while training faster on modern hardware. The results suggest that attention-centric architectures are a promising alternative for machine translation and possibly for other sequence modeling tasks.
\end{abstract}
