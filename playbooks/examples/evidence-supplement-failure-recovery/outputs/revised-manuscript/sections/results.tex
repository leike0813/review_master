\section{Results}
\subsection{Main Results}
The multimodal encoder improves separation because contextual fusion reduces confusion between visually similar stress patterns while preserving spectral contrast cues.

\subsection{Ablation Discussion}
Removing either the image branch or the spectral branch weakens performance, indicating that the gain comes from complementary cross-modal evidence rather than a single dominant stream.

\subsection{Seed Stability Experiment}
Following the reviewer request, we added a five-seed stability experiment. Across five random seeds, the mean macro-F1 remains stable and the spread stays narrow enough to support the robustness claim.
