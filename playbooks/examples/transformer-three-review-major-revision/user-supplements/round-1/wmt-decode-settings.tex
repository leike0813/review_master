\paragraph{Replication details.}
For WMT 2014 English-German we train on approximately 4.5M sentence pairs with shared byte-pair encoding and a vocabulary of roughly 37k tokens. For WMT 2014 English-French we use approximately 36M sentence pairs with a 32k word-piece vocabulary. Base-model results are reported after averaging the final 5 checkpoints written at 10-minute intervals, while big-model results average the final 20 checkpoints. During inference we use beam search with beam size 4, length penalty 0.6, and a maximum output length of input length plus 50 with early stopping when possible.
